Fitness AI Agent 🏋️‍♀️
A personalized fitness and nutrition tracking application powered by AI. This agent helps you log your daily intake, monitor your physical metrics, and provides smart insights to help you reach your health goals.

Features
Personal Profile Management: Track your age, weight, height, and gender to receive tailored recommendations.

Daily Logging: Easily record your daily calorie, protein, and water intake.

AI-Powered Insights: Uses Google Gemini to analyze your progress and offer health tips.

Cloud Integration: Your data is securely stored in Supabase, allowing you to access it from anywhere.

Tech Stack
Frontend/UI: Streamlit

Database: Supabase (PostgreSQL)

AI Engine: Google Gemini API

Language: Python
